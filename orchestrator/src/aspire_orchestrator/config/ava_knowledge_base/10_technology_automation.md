## Cybersecurity for Small Businesses

### Threat landscape (2025-2026)
- **43% of cyberattacks target small businesses** — they are seen as soft targets
- **60% of small businesses** that suffer a significant cyberattack close within 6 months
- **Average cost of a data breach** for businesses under 500 employees: $3.31 million (IBM 2025)
- **Ransomware attacks on SMBs** increased 150% from 2023 to 2025

### Minimum security measures
- **Multi-factor authentication (MFA)** on all business accounts — non-negotiable in 2026
- **Password manager** for the team (Bitwarden free, 1Password $8/user/month)
- **Automatic software updates** enabled on all devices
- **Business-grade antivirus/endpoint protection** ($3-8/device/month)
- **Regular backups** following 3-2-1 rule (3 copies, 2 media types, 1 offsite)
- **Cyber insurance** ($500-2,500/year for businesses under 50 employees, covers $1M+ in breach costs)
- **Employee training:** 90% of breaches start with phishing; quarterly awareness training reduces risk by 70%

## Systems Integration

### The connected stack
The goal is eliminating double-entry and manual data transfer between systems. Every disconnected system costs 2-5 hours/week in manual work and introduces errors.

**Ideal data flow:** Lead captured (CRM) -> Estimate created (FSM) -> Job scheduled (dispatch) -> Work completed (field app) -> Invoice generated (accounting) -> Payment collected (payment processor) -> Revenue recorded (books)

### Integration priorities
1. **Accounting <-> Invoicing:** Automatic sync eliminates reconciliation errors (QuickBooks + Jobber sync natively)
2. **CRM <-> Scheduling:** Lead becomes job without re-entering data
3. **Dispatch <-> Field app:** Crew gets job details, customer info, directions on their phone
4. **Invoicing <-> Payment:** Customer pays from the invoice link (reduces DSO by 10-15 days)
5. **All systems <-> Reporting:** Dashboard shows revenue, job count, close rate, cash position in real time

### Integration tools
- **Zapier:** $20-70/month, connects 6,000+ apps, no coding required
- **Make (Integromat):** $9-29/month, more complex automations
- **Native integrations:** Always preferred over third-party connectors (faster, more reliable)

## Mobile-First Field Operations

In 2026, 84% of field service workers use a smartphone as their primary work device. Any system that requires a desktop computer for field work is obsolete.

### Essential mobile capabilities
- View daily schedule with job details, customer info, and navigation
- Clock in/out with GPS verification
- Access job history and customer notes on-site
- Capture before/after photos linked to the job
- Collect customer signatures digitally
- Generate and send invoices from the field
- Accept credit card payments on-site (Square, Stripe, or FSM-integrated processing)

### Mobile payment adoption
- **73% of customers** prefer paying by card or digital payment in 2025-2026
- **Average days-to-payment** drops from 28 days (mailed invoice) to 3 days (mobile payment at completion)
- **Processing fees:** 2.6-2.9% + $0.10-0.30 per transaction (cost of doing business — never absorb as a reason to avoid accepting cards)
