ADAM INDUSTRY RESEARCH TEMPLATES

When researching a specific trade or industry, use these templates to know WHAT to research. Each template lists the key questions that matter for that industry.

---

LANDSCAPING

Market stream:
- Commercial vs residential demand in [city]
- Top landscaping competitors and what they charge
- Property management companies that hire landscapers
- Seasonal demand patterns (peak months vs slow months)

Operations stream:
- Equipment needed: mowers, edgers, blowers, trailers, trucks
- Licensing requirements by state (landscape contractor license)
- Insurance requirements (general liability, workers comp)
- Crew size and roles (foreman, laborers, equipment operator)
- Typical startup investment range

Revenue stream:
- Hourly rates for commercial maintenance
- Average annual contract value for commercial clients
- Per-visit vs monthly contract pricing
- Revenue per crew per day
- Break-even analysis (how many accounts to cover costs)

---

PAINTING

Market stream:
- Residential vs commercial demand in [city]
- Top painting contractors and their pricing
- Property managers and GCs who hire painters
- Seasonal patterns (exterior vs interior seasons)

Operations stream:
- Equipment: sprayers, rollers, brushes, ladders, scaffolding, drop cloths
- Licensing requirements by state
- Insurance (general liability, bonding)
- Crew structure (lead painter, helpers)
- Vehicle and storage needs

Revenue stream:
- Per square foot rates (interior vs exterior)
- Room-based pricing for residential
- Commercial bid pricing strategies
- Material markup percentages
- Revenue per painter per day
- Subcontractor vs employee economics

---

PLUMBING

Market stream:
- Service call demand in [city]
- Top plumbing companies and their pricing
- Emergency vs scheduled service demand
- New construction vs repair/maintenance split

Operations stream:
- Truck stock inventory and cost
- Licensing (master plumber, journeyman requirements)
- Insurance and bonding requirements
- Common tools and diagnostic equipment
- Apprenticeship pipeline

Revenue stream:
- Hourly rates (standard vs emergency)
- Average service call ticket
- Water heater replacement pricing
- Maintenance agreement models
- Revenue per technician per year
- First-call completion rate targets

---

HVAC

Market stream:
- Seasonal demand patterns (summer cooling vs winter heating)
- Top HVAC companies in [city]
- New construction vs retrofit/replacement market
- Energy efficiency trends and regulations

Operations stream:
- Equipment and diagnostic tools
- EPA 608 certification and state licensing
- Insurance requirements
- Seasonal workforce management
- Vehicle fleet needs

Revenue stream:
- Diagnostic fee and service call rates
- Equipment installation pricing (by tonnage and SEER rating)
- Maintenance contract pricing and retention rates
- Counter-seasonal revenue strategies
- Revenue per technician targets

---

PALLET AND WAREHOUSE

Market stream:
- GMA pallet demand in [city]
- Major buyers: food distributors, pharma, 3PLs, export companies
- New vs recycled pallet market dynamics
- Regional logistics corridors

Operations stream:
- Warehouse space requirements and lease rates
- Pallet building equipment (nail guns, saws, jigs)
- Forklift and material handling equipment
- ISPM-15 heat treatment requirements for export
- Staffing: builders, forklift operators, supervisor

Revenue stream:
- New pallet pricing (48x40 GMA standard)
- Recycled pallet buy/repair/sell margins
- Volume thresholds for profitability
- Customer contract structures (weekly, monthly)
- Break-even volume calculations

---

GENERAL CONTRACTOR

Market stream:
- Construction activity and permit data in [city]
- Top GCs and their specialties
- Residential remodel vs commercial buildout demand
- Subcontractor availability

Operations stream:
- Licensing and registration requirements
- Insurance (general liability, builders risk, bonding)
- Project management tools and processes
- Subcontractor recruitment and management
- Safety and OSHA compliance

Revenue stream:
- Gross margin targets by project type
- Subcontractor markup standards
- Change order revenue contribution
- Revenue per project manager
- Cash flow management (progress billing, retainage)

---

GENERAL (ANY INDUSTRY)

When the industry is not in the templates above, research these universal categories:

Market stream:
- Demand indicators in [city] for this service/product
- Existing competitors and their pricing
- Target customer profile
- Market trends and growth signals

Operations stream:
- Startup requirements (licenses, insurance, equipment)
- Staffing needs and labor costs
- Physical space or vehicle requirements
- Key vendor/supplier relationships

Revenue stream:
- Typical pricing models for this industry
- Average transaction/contract value
- Revenue per employee or unit benchmarks
- Break-even analysis framework
- Growth path from startup to scale
