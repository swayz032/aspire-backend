# Playbook: Accounts Receivable + Cashflow Hygiene

## Purpose
Reduce cash stress by tightening AR and improving predictability (proposal-only; not tax/legal advice).

## Use when
- invoices overdue
- cash anxiety / runway unclear
- customer payment delays

## Moves (in order)
1) **AR clarity**
- List invoices by age bucket (0–15, 16–30, 31–60, 60+).

2) **Follow-up cadence**
- Draft a 3-touch sequence: friendly reminder → firm reminder → final notice.

3) **Terms tightening**
- Draft updated terms: deposit, net-7/14, late fees (as a draft; user reviews with counsel if needed).

4) **Collections escalation rules**
- Define thresholds: when to pause work, when to require prepay.

5) **Runway dashboard**
- Minimum: cash on hand, weekly burn, expected receipts next 14/30 days.

## Output pattern (notes)
- AR snapshot (if available)
- One NBA (who to follow up today)
- Draft follow-up message(s)
- Policy suggestion (terms) as a draft
