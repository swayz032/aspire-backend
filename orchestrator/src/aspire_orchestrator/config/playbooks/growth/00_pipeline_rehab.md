# Playbook: Pipeline Rehab (7-day sprint)

## Purpose
Restore predictable revenue motion when leads/deals are dry or stalled.

## Use when
- "No leads", "pipeline is dead", "deals stalled"
- Revenue volatility or missed targets

## Sprint plan (proposal-only)
Day 1: **Inventory**
- List active leads/deals; label stage + next step + last touch date.

Day 2: **Reactivation**
- Draft 10 high-quality follow-ups to warm leads.

Day 3: **Offer clarity**
- Rewrite offer in one sentence; draft a short pitch email + landing blurb.

Day 4: **Referral push**
- Draft referral request to top customers.

Day 5: **Outbound batch**
- Draft outreach sequence to a narrow ICP list.

Day 6: **Objection bank**
- Compile top objections; draft replies + proof assets to answer them.

Day 7: **Review + adjust**
- Measure replies booked/calls scheduled; revise the weakest step.

## Output pattern (notes)
- Current pipeline signal (what’s known/unknown)
- Day 1 NBA
- Drafts to generate (Eli/Adam)
- Measurement checkpoint
