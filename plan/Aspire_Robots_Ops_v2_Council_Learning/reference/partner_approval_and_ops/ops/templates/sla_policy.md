# Minimal SLA / Support Policy (Starter)

## Support hours
- Initial: business hours (define timezone) + emergency escalation for critical incidents.

## Response targets (starter)
- P0 (payroll/transfer execution broken, security incident): acknowledge within 1 hour
- P1 (provider outage, webhook backlog): acknowledge within 4 hours
- P2 (user-impacting bug with workaround): acknowledge within 1 business day

## Escalation
- P0: flip to APPROVAL_ONLY or DISABLED if needed, notify impacted tenants, start incident channel.

## Evidence
- Keep receipts + replay bundle for all P0/P1 incidents.
